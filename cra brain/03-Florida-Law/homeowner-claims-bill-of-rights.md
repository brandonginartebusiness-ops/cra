# Florida Homeowner Claims Bill of Rights

## Statute

**Florida Statute §627.7142**

Source: [FL CFO Guide PDF](https://myfloridacfo.com/docs-sf/insurance-consumer-advocate-libraries/ica-documents/homeowner-claims-bill-of-rights-guide.pdf)

## Key rights guaranteed

| Right | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Hire a public adjuster | Insurers cannot prohibit PA representation |
| Demand appraisal | Quasi-arbitration over settlement dollar amounts (disputes only) |
| File DFS complaint | Call 1-877-693-5236 or [assistcon.myfloridacfo.gov](https://assistcon.myfloridacfo.gov) |
| Free mediation | DFS-administered for residential property disputes |
| Interest on late payments | If insurer misses 60-day payment deadline |

## Statute of limitations

Breach of contract suits: **5 years** (§95.11(2)(e))

> Note: this differs from the 1-year claim filing deadline imposed by [[03-Florida-Law/sb-2a-2022|SB 2-A]] — the SOL applies to breach of contract, not initial claim filing.

## Free mediation program

Source: [FL CFO Mediation Page](https://www.myfloridacfo.com/division/consumers/mediation)

DFS offers free mediation for residential property insurance disputes. This is a significant right that many policyholders don't know about — and it's a valuable content topic for CRA's blog.
