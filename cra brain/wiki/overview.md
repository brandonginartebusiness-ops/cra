## What is CRA?

**Claim Remedy Adjusters** is a Florida-based public adjusting firm that advocates for policyholders — homeowners and commercial property owners — when filing property insurance claims. Unlike insurance company adjusters (who represent the carrier), CRA works exclusively for the insured to maximize their claim settlement.

**Services:** roof claims, storm/hurricane damage, water damage, fire/smoke damage, mold.
**Geography:** Florida.
**Website:** claimremedyadjusters.com
