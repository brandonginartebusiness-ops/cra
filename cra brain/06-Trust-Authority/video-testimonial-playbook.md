# Video Testimonial Playbook

## Opportunity

Only **~20% of competitors** produce video content. Video testimonials are a major gap CRA can exploit for differentiation.

## Recording workflow

**When:** At the settlement moment — peak satisfaction, highest emotional impact
**Where:** At the client's property when possible (adds visual authenticity)
**Structure:** Problem → Solution → Result

## Format specs

| Use | Length |
|-----|--------|
| Social media clips | 60–90 seconds |
| Website testimonials | 2–3 minutes |

## Hosting strategy

| Platform | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Wistia** or **Vimeo Pro** | Website hosting — no competitor ads displayed |
| **YouTube** | Separate channel for SEO discovery |

Do NOT embed YouTube directly on the website — YouTube shows competitor ads in embedded players.

## Video content ideas (beyond testimonials)

- "What is a Public Adjuster?" — educational explainer for top-of-funnel
- Claims process walkthrough — reduces anxiety for potential clients
- Damage assessment narration — shows expertise in action
- Settlement reveal stories — dramatic, highly shareable
- Hurricane preparedness tips — seasonal, positions CRA as community resource
- FAQ answer series — repurposes FAQ content, supports [[04-SEO-Strategy/schema-markup|FAQ schema]]
- Behind-the-scenes day-in-the-life — humanizes the brand
