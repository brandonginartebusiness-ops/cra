# Google Review Strategy

## The numbers

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| CRA current reviews | ~6 |
| Industry leaders | 40–200+ |
| Reviews as % of local ranking factors | **15-17%** (Whitespark) |
| Revenue increase: 200+ reviews vs. 82 avg | **100%+ revenue increase** |
| Review velocity in 2026 | Recent reviews weighted more than lifetime total |

## Target

**2–3 new Google reviews per week**, sustained.

## Request workflow

1. **At moment of settlement** — peak satisfaction, ask for review in person
2. Send direct Google review link via **text message within 24 hours**
3. Create **QR code** for business cards and printed leave-behinds
4. Implement automated **post-settlement email sequence** with review request

## Response strategy

- Respond to **every review within 24 hours**
- Naturally incorporate keywords in responses:
  > "Thank you for trusting us with your **water damage claim in Miami Lakes**. We're so glad we could recover $X for you..."
- Never be defensive in negative responses (currently no negative reviews)

## Tools

- **Birdeye**: https://birdeye.com/
- **Podium** — SMS-based review requests
- **GatherUp** — automated review sequences
- **BrightLocal** — review monitoring
