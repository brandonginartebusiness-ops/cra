## Why pursue accreditation

- **80% of consumers** trust BBB-accredited businesses more
- Accredited = clickable BBB seal on website
- Moves rating toward A+ (competitor [[02-Competitors/all-claims-usa|All Claims USA]] has A+)
- BBB directory listing gets indexed by Google — citation value

## How to get accredited

Application: https://www.bbb.org/get-accredited
- Application fee: ~$75
- Annual dues: $300–$1,200 (based on business size)
