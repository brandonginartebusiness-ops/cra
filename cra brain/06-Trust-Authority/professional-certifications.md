# Professional Certifications

## Xactimate

The industry-standard claims estimation software. **[[02-Competitors/all-claims-usa|All Claims USA]]** explicitly markets Xactimate proficiency as a differentiator.

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Cost | $289–$569/month |
| Used by | Virtually all PA firms (but few market it explicitly) |
| Differentiator | Position as "we speak the insurer's language" |

Using and marketing Xactimate proficiency signals that CRA produces professional, industry-standard estimates that are harder for insurers to dispute.

## HAAG Certified Inspector

Specialized certification for roof inspections. Highly relevant given roof claims are one of CRA's core services.

- Recognized by insurance industry as a mark of roof inspection expertise
- Provides credibility in roof damage claim disputes

## IICRC Certifications

Institute of Inspection Cleaning and Restoration Certification:
- **WRT** — Water Damage Restoration Technician
- **ASD** — Applied Structural Drying
- **AMRT** — Applied Microbial Remediation (mold)

IICRC certifications demonstrate deep technical knowledge of water and mold damage — relevant to CRA's water damage and mold claim services.
