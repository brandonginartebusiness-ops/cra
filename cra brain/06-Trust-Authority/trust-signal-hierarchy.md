# Trust Signal Hierarchy

## Tier 1 — Must-have immediately

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| FAPIA member badge | Join [[06-Trust-Authority/fapia-membership|FAPIA]] first |
| FL License W549958 prominently displayed | Already have — make more visible |
| Google Reviews widget with star rating | Embed on homepage |
| "Licensed, Bonded & Insured" badge | Add to header/footer |
| "No Recovery, No Fee" guarantee | Already have — ensure above fold |

## Tier 2 — Add within 90 days

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| BBB Accredited Business seal (clickable) | Pursue [[06-Trust-Authority/bbb-accreditation|BBB accreditation]] |
| NAPIA member badge | Join [[06-Trust-Authority/napia-membership|NAPIA]] |
| Dollar amount recovered counter | "**$X Million+ Recovered**" — update regularly |
| Years of experience display | Add to About page and homepage |
| Number of claims handled | Add with stats |

## Tier 3 — Build over 6–12 months

| Signal | Requires |
|--------|---------|
| CPPA/SPPA designation | 5-10 years experience |
| "As Seen On" media logos | Press outreach |
| Industry awards | Submit to FL PA industry awards |
| Before/after case study gallery | Accumulate and document wins |

## Where to place trust signals

- **Header**: Phone, license number, "Licensed & Bonded"
- **Above fold**: Google rating, FAPIA badge, "No Fee Unless Paid"
- **Adjacent to every CTA/form**: FAPIA, BBB seal, Google rating, license
- **Footer**: All badges, license, bond, social links
- **About page**: Full credential stack
