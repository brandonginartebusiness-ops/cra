# FAPIA Membership — #1 Trust Signal for Florida PAs

## What is FAPIA?

**Florida Association of Public Insurance Adjusters**
Website: https://www.fapia.net/
Members: 700+

FAPIA is the premier industry association for Florida public adjusters. Consumers searching for a PA are advised to "ask for a FAPIA ID card" — it's the credibility signal that distinguishes legitimate PAs.

## Benefits

- Consumer-facing **member directory** — a source of inbound leads
- **CE courses** — satisfy [[03-Florida-Law/pa-licensing-requirements|24-hour CE requirement]]
- Legislative advocacy
- Industry networking and conferences
- FAPIA member badge for website
- Credibility with referral sources (attorneys, contractors, real estate agents)

## How to join

- Application: https://fapia.memberclicks.net/public-adjuster-membership
- Phone: 866-235-6489

## Priority level

**Highest priority.** Join FAPIA before almost anything else. Every top competitor has FAPIA membership displayed. CRA's absence from the FAPIA directory is a significant trust and visibility gap.
