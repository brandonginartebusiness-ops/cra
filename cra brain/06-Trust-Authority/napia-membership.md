# NAPIA Membership — National Credibility

## What is NAPIA?

**National Association of Public Insurance Adjusters**
Website: https://www.napia.com/

NAPIA is the national counterpart to [[06-Trust-Authority/fapia-membership|FAPIA]]. Membership provides national credibility and access to the only professional designations designed specifically for public adjusters.

## Professional designations

Offered through **The Institutes**:

| Designation | Requirements | Meaning |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| **CPPA** — Certified Professional Public Adjuster | 5+ years experience | Mid-career credibility signal |
| **SPPA** — Senior Professional Public Adjuster | 10+ years experience | Senior-level expertise |

These are the only professional designations specifically designed for PAs. Displaying CPPA or SPPA after a name signals years of verified experience and commitment to the profession.

## Priority level

**High priority (within 90 days).** NAPIA membership is secondary to [[06-Trust-Authority/fapia-membership|FAPIA]] for Florida-specific credibility but important for national recognition and the designation pathway.
