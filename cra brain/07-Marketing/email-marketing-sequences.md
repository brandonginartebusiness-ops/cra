# Email Marketing Sequences

## New Lead Drip Sequence

| Day | Email |
|-----|-------|
| Immediately | Confirmation — "We received your inquiry, here's what happens next" |
| Day 1 | Case study — share a relevant dollar recovery story |
| Day 3 | Video testimonial (link) |
| Day 5 | "Your Rights as a Florida Policyholder" — [[03-Florida-Law/homeowner-claims-bill-of-rights|Bill of Rights]] summary |
| Day 7 | Claim filing deadline urgency — [[03-Florida-Law/sb-2a-2022|SB 2-A]] 1-year window |
| Day 14 | Follow-up — "Still thinking it over?" |
| Day 30 | Final check-in |

## Active Client Sequence

| Trigger | Email |
|---------|-------|
| Signed contract | Welcome and onboarding — what to expect, your CRA contact |
| Weekly | Status update — claim progress summary |
| Key milestones | Explanation of what just happened and what's next |
| Settlement | Review request (see [[06-Trust-Authority/google-review-strategy|Google Review Strategy]]) |
| Post-settlement | Thank you + referral ask (organic — no compensation) |

## Seasonal Campaigns

| Month | Campaign |
|-------|---------|
| May–June | Hurricane preparation — checklist, policy review reminder |
| June–November | Storm tracking — rapid response offer if a storm hits |
| December–February | Pipe freeze prevention for FL homes |
| March–May | Spring maintenance — roof inspection reminder |

## Tools

- **ClaimWizard** or **Claim Titan** for PA-specific CRM with built-in sequences — see [[07-Marketing/crm-recommendations|CRM Recommendations]]
- **Mailchimp** or **ActiveCampaign** for standalone email marketing
