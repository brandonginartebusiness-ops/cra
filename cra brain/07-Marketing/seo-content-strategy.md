# SEO Content Strategy

## The business case

Firms with blogs generate **67% more monthly leads** than those without (DemandMetric).

~80% of top FL PA competitors publish regular blog content. Tutwiler & Associates ([[02-Competitors/tutwiler-associates|publicadjuster.com]]) runs the strongest content operation in the industry.

## Output targets

- **2–4 blog posts per month**
- Each post: **1,000–2,000 words**
- Every post targets a specific keyword from [[04-SEO-Strategy/keyword-hierarchy|Tier 4]] (educational) or feeds a [[04-SEO-Strategy/site-architecture|service hub]]

## Hub-and-spoke model

**Hubs** = Service pages (high commercial intent, target Tier 1-3 keywords)
**Spokes** = Blog posts (educational intent, target Tier 4 keywords)

Spokes link internally to their hub:
- Blog: "What to Do After Hurricane Damage" → links to `/services/hurricane-wind-damage/`
- Blog: "Hidden Signs of Water Damage" → links to `/services/water-damage/`
- Blog: "Is My Insurance Claim Underpaid?" → links to `/services/underpaid-insurance-claims/`

Internal links from multiple spokes to a hub build **topical authority** for that hub page.
