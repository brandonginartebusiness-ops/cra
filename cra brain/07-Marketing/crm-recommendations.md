# CRM & Software Recommendations

## PA-specific CRM options

| Tool | URL | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| **ClaimWizard** | https://claimwizard.com/ | Gold standard for PA workflow management |
| **Claim Titan** | https://claimtitan.com/ | Built by a PA, configurable workflows |
| **AdjustCRM** | https://www.adjustcrm.com/ | Modern UI with AI automations |
| **Brelly** | https://brelly.com/public-adjuster-claims-software/ | AI-powered claims software |

**Recommendation:** Evaluate ClaimWizard first — it's the most widely used by FL PA firms.

## Estimating software

| Tool | URL | Cost | Notes |
|------|-----|------|-------|
| **Xactimate** | xactware.com | $289–$569/month | Industry standard — insurers use it too |

Xactimate is what insurance companies use to estimate claims. PA firms that use it produce estimates insurers can't easily dispute and signal professional credibility. See [[06-Trust-Authority/professional-certifications|Professional Certifications]].
