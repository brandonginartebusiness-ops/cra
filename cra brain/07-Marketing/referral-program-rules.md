# Referral Program — What's Legal vs. Prohibited

> **See [[03-Florida-Law/referral-compensation-rules|Referral Compensation Rules]] for the legal detail.**

## The hard line

**FL §626.854(12-13)**: CRA cannot pay any non-PA for referrals. Period.

This eliminates: paid referral fees to contractors, water mitigation companies, real estate agents, attorneys, roofers, or any other non-PA source.

Violations: $10,000-$20,000 per instance.

## Organic relationship building (what IS allowed)

These relationships are legal when no compensation agreement exists:

| Relationship | How to build |
|-------------|-------------|
| Attorneys (property/insurance) | [[06-Trust-Authority/fapia-membership|FAPIA]] conferences, bar association events, joint educational workshops |
| Real estate agents | Community networking events, shared educational content |
| Contractors | Provide excellent service to their mutual clients (reputation-based) |
| Water mitigation companies | Same — organic, no quid pro quo |
| Mortgage servicers | Lunch-and-learns explaining PA services |

## Community networking strategy

- Attend [[06-Trust-Authority/fapia-membership|FAPIA]] conferences and local chapter events
- Legal/real estate community events in Miami-Dade
- Co-host educational workshops with attorneys (both educate, no money changes hands)
- Miami Lakes Chamber of Commerce membership and events
- Nextdoor presence for neighborhood-level trust
