# Common Claims in Florida

Sources: [III Facts & Statistics](https://www.iii.org/fact-statistic/facts-statistics-homeowners-and-renters-insurance) · [NFIP Data](https://nfipservices.floodsmart.gov/reports-flood-insurance-data) · [LexisNexis Home Trends](https://risk.lexisnexis.com/about-us/press-room/press-release/20241016-home-trends-report) · [Insurify FL Flood](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/news/florida-nfip-flood-claims/)

## Claim type frequency and payouts

| Claim Type | % of All Claims | Average Payout |
|-----------|-----------------|---------------|
| Wind and hail | **42.5%** | **$14,747** |
| Water damage | **27.6%** | **$13,954** |
| Fire and lightning | Smaller % | **$88,170** (highest avg) |

## Florida-specific data points

### Water damage
- FL OIR 2024: **165,031 closed water claims** statewide
- Water is the highest-frequency claim type in Florida after wind

### Fire and lightning
- Florida led the nation with **6,003 lightning claims** in 2023
- Fire/lightning has the highest average payout of any claim type

### Sinkhole claims
- Approximately **6,500 Floridians** file sinkhole-related claims annually
- Florida-specific risk — see [[02-Competitors/pinnacle-claim-services|Pinnacle's sinkhole specialty page]]

### Flood insurance
- Florida has **1.7 million NFIP flood policyholders** with $452 billion in coverage
- Only **18% of Floridians** carry any flood insurance
- A large uninsured/underinsured gap exists

## Claims cycle time (2024)

- Claim filing to repair completion: **32.4 days** average (longest since 2008 per J.D. Power)
- First notice to final payment: **44+ days** average

## Relevance to CRA

- Wind/hail (42.5%) and water (27.6%) together = **70% of all claims** — CRA's core service targets are correct
- Fire at $88K average payout = highest dollar per claim — worth prioritizing
- Flood insurance gap = unmet need that CRA can address in content (public adjusters work on NFIP claims too)
- 6,500 annual sinkhole claims = worth a dedicated page
