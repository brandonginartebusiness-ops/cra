# Local SEO Execution Plan

Sources: [Search Engine Land — 90-Day Local SEO](https://searchengineland.com/local-seo-sprints-a-90-day-plan-for-service-businesses-in-2026-469059) · [Backlinko Local SEO Guide](https://backlinko.com/local-seo-guide) · [LocalMighty Best Practices](https://localmighty.com/blog/local-seo-best-practices/)

## Google Business Profile (GBP) — highest single impact action

### Setup
- Primary category: **"Public Adjuster"**
- Secondary categories: "Insurance Consultant," "Claims Adjuster"
- Complete every available field
- Upload 15+ real project/team photos

### Ongoing
- Post **weekly** with real project photos and updates
- Proactively populate the Q&A section with FAQs before customers ask
- Respond to **every review within 24 hours** (naturally incorporating keywords)

### Review velocity
Reviews account for **15-17% of local ranking factors** (Whitespark). In 2026, velocity (recent reviews) matters more than lifetime totals.

Target: **2-3 new Google reviews per week**

CRA has ~6 reviews. Industry leaders have 40-200+. See [[06-Trust-Authority/google-review-strategy|Google Review Strategy]] for the full playbook.
