# Keyword Targeting Hierarchy

## Tier 1 — High-intent service keywords
*Target with main service pages*

- "public adjuster Miami"
- "public adjuster near me"
- "public adjuster Florida"
- "best public adjuster Miami"
- "licensed public adjuster Miami-Dade County"

## Tier 2 — Damage-type keywords
*Target with individual damage service pages*

- "hurricane damage claim help Florida"
- "water damage claim help Florida"
- "roof damage insurance claim Florida"
- "fire damage claim help Florida"
- "mold damage insurance claim Florida"
- "pipe burst insurance claim Florida"

## Tier 3 — Problem/pain-point keywords
*Target with specialized landing pages and content*

- "underpaid insurance claim Florida"
- "denied insurance claim help Florida"
- "insurance adjuster lowball offer"
- "reopening insurance claim Florida"
- "supplemental claim Florida"

## Tier 4 — Educational/informational keywords
*Target with blog content*

- "how to file insurance claim Florida"
- "public adjuster cost Florida"
- "do I need a public adjuster"
- "public adjuster vs insurance adjuster"
- "signs your insurance claim is underpaid"

## Tier 5 — Hyper-local keywords
*Target with city-specific landing pages*

- "public adjuster Miami Lakes"
- "public adjuster Hialeah"
- "public adjuster Doral"
- "insurance claim help Broward County"
- "property damage claim Miami-Dade"

## People Also Ask — FAQ schema targets

Consistently surfacing in PAA boxes across these searches:
- "How much does a public adjuster charge in Florida?"
- "Is it worth hiring a public adjuster?"
- "What is the difference between an adjuster and a public adjuster?"

→ All three are ripe for [[04-SEO-Strategy/schema-markup|FAQPage schema]] and dedicated content.
