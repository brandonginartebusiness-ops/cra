## Differentiators

- **42+ years of experience** — central to all messaging
- Both [[06-Trust-Authority/fapia-membership|FAPIA]] and [[06-Trust-Authority/napia-membership|NAPIA]] membership
- Media appearances by spokesperson Mike Rump
- **Hurricane-specific landing pages** for Helene and Milton — they move fast after named storms
- Licensing in 23+ states signals major credibility

## Content strategy

Continental creates dedicated pages for each hurricane event as soon as storms hit. This is a smart SEO and conversion tactic — homeowners search for their specific storm name after damage.

## What CRA can learn

- Create dedicated hurricane/storm event pages quickly after major events
- Pursue dual FAPIA + NAPIA membership
- Develop a recognizable spokesperson or team
