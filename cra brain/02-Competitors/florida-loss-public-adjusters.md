## Differentiators

- **Proprietary claim tracking software** — sends automated weekly status updates to clients
- One of only 2 firms in the market with a client portal

## Why client portals matter

Client anxiety is one of the biggest pain points in insurance claims — people don't know what's happening with their claim, when to expect updates, or what they should be doing. Automated weekly status updates address this directly and reduce inbound support calls while improving client satisfaction.

## What CRA can learn

- A client portal or even simple automated email/SMS status updates would be a genuine differentiator
