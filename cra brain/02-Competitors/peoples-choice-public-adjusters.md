# People's Choice Public Adjusters

## Profile

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Website | https://tpcpublicadjusters.com |
| Location | Lake Worth, FL |

## Differentiators

- **Former insurance company adjusters** who "switched sides" — a powerful trust and credibility positioning story
- Uses [[05-Claims-Data/oppaga-study|OPPAGA statistics]] prominently: 574% and 747% higher payouts cited
- **Policy Companion** online tool — unique client-facing tool for policyholders

## Positioning analysis

"We used to work for them. Now we work for you." is a very strong positioning statement. Former insurer adjusters understand exactly how carriers think, what adjusters look for, and how to counter lowball offers. CRA should consider whether any team members have this background and can use it.

The OPPAGA stats (574%/747%) are compelling but come with important context — see [[05-Claims-Data/oppaga-study|OPPAGA Study]].

## What CRA can learn

- Lean into team credentials and origin story on the About page
- Use OPPAGA data with appropriate context
- An interactive "Policy Companion" type tool would be highly differentiating
