# Stellar Public Adjusting

## Profile

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Website | https://stellaradjusting.com |
| Location | Hollywood, FL |

## Differentiators

- **Free virtual claim inspections** — the only competitor offering this (major differentiator)
- Dramatic settlement success stories displayed prominently: **$266,000** and **$372,000** recoveries
- Dedicated Condo Claims page
- Dedicated Business Interruption page

## Content strategy

Stellar leads with big dollar amounts on case studies — similar to CRA's $18K→$147K example but even larger numbers. They show multiple settlement amounts on the homepage. This is proven conversion content.

## What CRA can learn

- Consider offering virtual inspections — no other competitor does this
- Build out condo claims and business interruption as separate service pages
- Accumulate and display more large settlement stories
