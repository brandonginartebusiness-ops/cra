# Pinnacle Claim Services

## Profile

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Website | https://pinnacleclaimservices.com |
| Location | Pompano Beach, FL |
| Live agents | 24/7 |

## Differentiators

- **Most aggressive local SEO strategy** of any Florida PA — dozens of city-specific landing pages across the state
- Sinkhole damage specialty content (rare niche — ~6,500 FL sinkhole claims/year)
- 24/7 live agents — a significant conversion and trust advantage

## Why this matters for CRA

Pinnacle demonstrates that city-page volume is a key SEO driver. Their dozens of pages targeting "[city] public adjuster" allow them to capture long-tail local searches statewide. CRA should replicate this for Miami-Dade and Broward at minimum. See [[04-SEO-Strategy/site-architecture|Site Architecture]].

## What CRA can learn

- Prioritize city-specific landing page creation (unique content, not template swaps)
- Consider 24/7 answering service — captures leads at all hours after storm events
- Sinkhole specialty could be worth a dedicated page (Florida-specific, uncontested by CRA)
