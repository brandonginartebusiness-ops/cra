About Claim Remedy Adjusters

Florida-licensed public adjusters focused on homeowners and property owners — never the insurance carrier.

Who we are

Based in Miami Lakes, Claim Remedy Adjusters brings more than 10 years of hands-on experience with residential and commercial property claims. As licensed Florida public adjusters, our loyalty is strictly with the policyholder.

When hurricanes, plumbing failures, or fires damage your property, the insurer sends adjusters who protect their interests. We document the full scope of damage, build a strong claim file, and negotiate so you are positioned for a fair settlement.

We believe Florida homeowners deserve clear communication, honest timelines, and advocacy at every step.

Our credentials

  • Licensed public adjusters in the State of Florida
  • 10+ years of industry experience
  • Serving Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach, Monroe, and Collier counties
