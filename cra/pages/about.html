About Us

Over a decade fighting for Florida homeowners

We exist for one reason: to make sure you're not left fighting your insurance company alone. We know the process, the policy language, and the tactics — and we use them for you.

Our story

Built from the ground up to level the playing field

Claim Remedy Adjusters was founded on a simple premise: when a homeowner suffers property damage, they deserve fair representation — not a complicated fight against an insurer with unlimited resources.

After more than 10 years of direct claim handling across Florida, we've seen every type of lowball offer, bad-faith delay, and unjust denial. We built this firm to fight back on your behalf.

Our approach

How we handle every claim

Policy-First Review

We start by reading your policy carefully — understanding your coverage, exclusions, and limits before we inspect anything.

Comprehensive Field Inspection

Our adjusters walk every inch of the affected property, capturing photographic evidence, measurements, and written observations.

Itemized Loss Documentation

We prepare a detailed proof of loss with line-item damage estimates aligned to current market replacement costs.

Strategic Negotiation

We handle all communication and negotiation with your carrier — using our documentation to push back on underpayments and denials.
