By claim type

Expert support for every loss type

From wind-driven rain to coastal flood scenarios — here is how we help on each claim.

🌪️

Hurricane & wind damage

As a hurricane damage public adjuster in Miami and across South Florida, we handle roof lifts, structural damage, fallen trees, and wind-driven rain. We document exterior and interior losses and push back on under-scoped repairs.

💧

Water damage & plumbing leaks

Burst pipes, cast iron failures, slab leaks, and long-term moisture issues. If you need water damage claim help in Broward County or anywhere we serve, we trace causation and capture damage the first walkthrough can miss.

🔥

Fire & smoke damage

Structure fires, electrical fires, and heavy smoke — we help with fire damage insurance claims in South Florida by addressing structure, contents, and code-related rebuild costs where policies allow.

🏠

Roof damage claims

Missing shingles, storm-related openings, and aging assemblies. From Miami Lakes to Palm Beach, we align scope with manufacturer specs and local building requirements so your roof claim reflects real repair costs.

🦠

Mold remediation claims

Mold resulting from leaks or flooding. We tie remediation to covered water events and document testing and removal where appropriate — critical for Florida homeowners dealing with humidity and repeat moisture.

🌊

Flood damage

NFIP and private flood coverages, rising water, and storm surge scenarios. We help Monroe and coastal clients organize proof of loss and resolve line-item disputes with clarity.
