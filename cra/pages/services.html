Roof Leaks & Wind Damage

Roof claims are routinely underpaid

Your roof is your home's first line of defense — and one of the most disputed claim categories. Insurers often classify damage as "wear and tear" to avoid full replacement costs. We document the true storm-related cause and fight for proper reimbursement.

  • Roof system documentation — decking, underlayment, flashing
  • Matching material and full replacement cost arguments
  • Water intrusion tied to roof compromise

Fire & Smoke Losses

Fire claims are more complex than they appear

Fire damage extends far beyond the visible burn zone. Smoke penetrates walls, HVAC systems, and belongings throughout the home. Suppression water causes secondary damage. Without thorough documentation, homeowners recover a fraction of their true losses.

  • Structural fire and burn damage documentation
  • Smoke and soot contamination throughout the property
  • Suppression water and secondary damage assessment
  • HVAC, ductwork, and hidden system damage
  • Personal property inventory and valuation
