Common questions

Everything you need to know.

A public adjuster is a licensed insurance professional who represents homeowners, not the insurance company, during the claims process. We document damage, file claims, and negotiate on your behalf to help you recover the maximum settlement allowed under your policy.
You pay absolutely nothing upfront. Our fee is a percentage of the settlement we recover for you. If we do not recover anything, you owe us nothing. That means our interests stay fully aligned with yours: we only win when you win.
Yes. Many homeowners come to us after their claim was underpaid or denied. We can reopen your claim, bring in additional documentation and evidence, and renegotiate with your insurer for a fair settlement.
We handle all types of residential property damage claims in Florida: hurricane and wind damage, water damage and pipe bursts, fire and smoke damage, roof claims, mold damage, and more. If your property is damaged and you have an insurance policy, we can help.
Every claim is different, but most claims settle within 30-90 days once we have filed all documentation. Complex or disputed claims may take longer, but we keep you informed at every step and push for the fastest possible resolution.
